Why Kombu Time?

Kombu Time was born out of the need for a no-fuss, straightforward kombucha brewing app. If you're tired of apps overloaded with features you don't need, Kombu Time is the perfect solution. It does exactly what you need—nothing more, nothing less.

All features are designed to enhance your brewing without adding complexity. History tracking, flavor notes, and tea types are completely optional. Use what helps you, ignore what doesn't.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Kombu Time free?

Yes! Kombu Time is completely free with no ads and no in-app purchases. All features are available to everyone.

Does Kombu Time require an internet connection?

No. All your data is stored locally on your device. No internet connection is needed, and no data is sent to external servers.

Can I export my brewing data?

Yes! You can export your complete brewing history as CSV or JSON files for your own analysis and record-keeping.

Which platforms are supported?

Kombu Time is available for iOS devices (iPhone and iPad) and Android devices.

Can I customize fermentation times?

Absolutely! You can set custom fermentation durations for both first and second fermentations to match your brewing preferences.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your data stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or share any personal information. No user accounts are required.
